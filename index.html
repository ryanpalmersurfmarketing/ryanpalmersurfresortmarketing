About Ryan

I've Stayed at the Resorts I Market For

Based in Kennebunk, Maine, I work with boutique surf resorts worldwide — from Fiji to Nicaragua, Indonesia to Morocco. My edge is simple: I've been a guest at the resorts I represent. I understand what makes someone book a surf trip, what keeps them coming back, and what they tell their friends afterward.

Most marketing agencies treat surf resorts like any other hospitality client. I don't. Every pitch angle, every email sequence, every SEO keyword I target is built around the specific psychology of the surf traveler — someone who plans trips around swell forecasts, values authenticity over polish, and books based on trust.

My focus is exclusively on surf resorts and camps. That means when I pitch a journalist at The Inertia, I know their audience. When I write your SEO content, I know exactly what your guests are searching for.
